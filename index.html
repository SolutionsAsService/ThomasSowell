Biography

Thomas Sowell is an American economist and social theorist who has written extensively on economics, history, and social policy. He is known for his rigorous scholarship and clear, accessible writing style. Sowell is a Senior Fellow at the Hoover Institution at Stanford University.

Notable Works

  • Basic Economics
  • Conflict of Visions
  • Wealth, Poverty and Politics
  • Discrimination and Disparities

Quotes

"Socialism in general has a record of failure so blatant that only an intellectual could ignore or evade it."
"The first lesson of economics is scarcity: there is never enough of anything to fully satisfy all those who want it."
